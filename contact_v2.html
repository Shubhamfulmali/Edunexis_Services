Before You Reach Out

Common Questions

How do I get started? +
Fill out the form above or email us at edunexis.service@gmail.com with your requirements and deadline. We'll respond with a clear quote within 4-6 hours.
What information should I include? +
Include the type of project, your university or company, the deadline, any rubric or requirements, and any files or materials we need to reference. More detail = faster quote.
How are payments made? +
We accept UPI, bank transfer, PayPal, and Wise. Payment details are shared after your project is confirmed. No upfront payment for first-time clients on small projects.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer unlimited revisions first. In exceptional cases where we truly cannot meet requirements, a partial or full refund is arranged. We prioritize satisfaction above all.
Can I request urgent delivery? +
Yes! Same-day and next-day delivery available. Just mention your deadline and we'll confirm. An urgent fee may apply depending on project size.
Is my project kept confidential? +
Absolutely. Your name, university, business info, and project details are never shared. NDAs available on request at no charge.
